About Me

I’m passionate about transforming raw data into clear insights that drive smarter decisions. With a mix of technical skills and financial background, I enjoy exploring datasets, finding patterns, and building solutions that connect numbers with business impact.

My path in data analytics began at General Electric (Finance Intern), where I automated Excel workflows to clean server-downloaded data and build dynamic order and sales reports. That’s when I realized the power of data to drive better decisions.

At GrupoRPP, Peru’s leading media conglomerate, I transformed financial data into actionable insights: customized expense reports by department, drill-down cost analyses, and automations that improved efficiency and supported strategic decision-making.

I lived and worked in Montreal (2022–2024), collaborating with multicultural, bilingual teams. That experience strengthened my English and French communication, adaptability, and stakeholder management in cross-cultural environments.

In 2025 I deepened my learning: Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn), SQL, and Power BI; while continuing to leverage that prior international experience. I bring a combination of financial rigor, curiosity, and a builder mindset.

What I’m looking for: a data-driven team where I can turn raw data into tangible business impact, combining analytical depth with practical, results-oriented execution.
